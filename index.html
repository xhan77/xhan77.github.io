Bio

I am a first year Master of Language Technologies student at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Yulia Tsvetkov. I am interested in Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, and Computational Social Science. Before coming to CMU, I was a member of Georgia Tech Computational Linguistics Lab, advised by Jacob Eisenstein. I also spent a summer at the NLP and CSS Lab of University of Colorado Boulder, advised by Chenhao Tan.
