Bio

I'm a PhD student in computer science and engineering at the University of Washington, advised by Yulia Tsvetkov. I'm generally interested in natural language processing and have worked on topics like model interpretability, diffusion language models, etc. Before UW, I was a Master of Language Technologies student at Carnegie Mellon University. Before CMU, I was an undergrad at Georgia Tech, advised by Jacob Eisenstein.
